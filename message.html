For You A

I know na I was losing you pero hindi ko talaga alam how to change things. Sinabi mo sakin “You deserve better” no ikaw dapat yon. You deserved someone who was truly ready na can give you everything without hesitation.

I decided to stay away and naisip ko na yun yung best way to give you space to find someone who could treat you better. I love you which is why I chose to let go and stop communicating with you, hanggang now hindi pa ako maka move on sayo and I haven’t been able to meet anyone new dahil I still have feelings for you.

Sa ngayon I’m focusing on fixing myself first, becoming someone who’s truly ready. Maybe in the future, if our paths cross again, you will have the best version of me not the better but the best the one who’s ready to risk everything for you and give the best and genuine love that you deserve.
